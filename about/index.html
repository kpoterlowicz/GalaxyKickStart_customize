GalaxyKickStart

GalaxyKickStart is an Ansible playbook designed to help you get one or more production-ready Galaxy servers based on Ubuntu within minutes, and to maintain these servers.

Required ansible version >= 2.1.2.0

Optionally, instances can be pre-loaded with tools and workflows.

The playbook has been tested on

  • Cloud Machines
  • Vagrant Boxes
  • Physical Servers
  • Docker.

GalaxyKickStart has been developed at the ARTbio platform and contains roles developed by the Galaxy team.

List of roles included in this playbook

  • ensure_postrgesql_up
  • galaxy-extras role
  • galaxy-tools role
  • galaxy-os role
  • galaxy role
  • galaxy-trackster role
  • natefoo-postgresql_objects
  • miniconda-role
